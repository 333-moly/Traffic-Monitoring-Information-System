About Traffic Navigation

Traffic Navigation is designed specifically for day scholar students of KSRM College of Engineering, Kadapa, who commute daily from various parts of the city and nearby towns.

Our system provides real-time traffic monitoring and alerts to help students plan their commute effectively and reach college on time, avoiding unnecessary delays caused by traffic congestion, accidents, or roadwork.

Built by students, for students — this platform aims to make daily commuting stress-free and predictable.
